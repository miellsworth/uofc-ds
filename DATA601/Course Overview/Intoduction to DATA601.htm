DS and Analytics at UCalgary

  • Data science is the application of computing, statistics, and analytic sciences to address data-driven challenges, and to acquire a better understanding of the world in which we currently live.
     
  • Analytics is “about understanding the tools available to provide better decision-making support,” and focuses more on “the application of big data rather than…the manipulation and maintenance of the data itself.”

It's about gaining actionable insights from data

Our focus

Data Science Environment

  • Python and R are now widely used in data science. They are well documented and supported and have lots of libraries for various tasks in the data science workflow.
     
  • Our language of choice for DATA 601 is Python 3.
     
  • We'll make use of the Jupyter notebook environment.
     
  • We'll use the Anaconda platform to manage our data science environment.
